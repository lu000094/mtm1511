Programs & Classes

Free programs for learners of every age

Our library offers a wide range of free programs throughout the year. From early literacy to digital skills, there is something for everyone. All programs are free with your library card.

Featured Programs

Kids

Story Time

Interactive storytelling, songs, and crafts for children ages 3 to 6. Held every Wednesday morning in the Children's Room.

Adults

Book Club

Join our monthly book discussions. We read a variety of genres from classic literature to contemporary fiction.

All Ages

Digital Skills Workshop

Learn the basics of computers, email, and online safety. Beginner-friendly sessions with one-on-one help.

Teens

Teen Art Club

Express your creativity with our teen art club. Each month features a new medium, from sketching to digital design.

Seniors

Senior Social Hour

Friendly gatherings every Thursday afternoon with coffee, games, and conversation. Newcomers always welcome.

Adults

Job Search Help

Free resume reviews, interview practice, and career counselling. Drop-in sessions every Tuesday.

How to Register

Most of our programs are drop-in style and do not require registration. For programs with limited space, you can register online, by phone, or in person at the front desk.

All programs are free. You do not need a library card to attend, but a free card is a great way to borrow materials related to your program.
